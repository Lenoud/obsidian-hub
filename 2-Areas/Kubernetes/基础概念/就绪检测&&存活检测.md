# 就绪检测&&存活检测

就绪检测&&存活检测相关技术笔记。

http方式

spec.containers.livenessProbe

spec.containers.readinessProbe

```yaml
          livenessProbe:
            failureThreshold: 3
            httpGet:
              path: /index.html
              port: 80
              scheme: HTTP
            initialDelaySeconds: 20
            periodSeconds: 10
            successThreshold: 1
            timeoutSeconds: 1
          readinessProbe:
            failureThreshold: 3
            httpGet:
              path: /index.html
              port: 80
              scheme: HTTP
            initialDelaySeconds: 20
            periodSeconds: 10
            successThreshold: 1
            timeoutSeconds: 1
```

tcp方式

```yaml
          livenessProbe:
            failureThreshold: 3
            initialDelaySeconds: 60
            periodSeconds: 10
            successThreshold: 1
            tcpSocket:
              port: 80
            timeoutSeconds: 1
          ports:
            - containerPort: 80
              hostPort: 9654
              name: http
              protocol: TCP
          readinessProbe:
            failureThreshold: 3
            initialDelaySeconds: 20
            periodSeconds: 20
            successThreshold: 1
            tcpSocket:
              port: 80
            timeoutSeconds: 1
```

command方式

```yaml
          livenessProbe:
            exec:
              command:
                - ls /usr
                - ls /mnt
            failureThreshold: 3
            initialDelaySeconds: 20
            periodSeconds: 10
            successThreshold: 1
            timeoutSeconds: 1
          readinessProbe:
            exec:
              command:
                - ls /mnt
                - ls /usr
            failureThreshold: 3
            initialDelaySeconds: 20
            periodSeconds: 10
            successThreshold: 1
            timeoutSeconds: 1
```
